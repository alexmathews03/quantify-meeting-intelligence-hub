# QUANTIFY: Hackathon Demo Script

*Aim for about 2 minutes. Keep the energy high and focus on the visual impact of the tool!*

---

### Phase 1: The Problem & Ingestion (0:00 - 0:30)
*(Start on the main Landing Page or Login Page. Move your mouse as you speak.)*

**"Hi everyone, I'm Alex, and this is QUANTIFY: The intelligent solution to the 'Double-Work' cycle."**

**"Organizations waste hundreds of hours re-reading 20-page meeting transcripts because task assignments and critical decisions get lost in the noise. QUANTIFY solves this by transforming raw dialogue into structured intelligence instantly."**

*(Log in. You should be on the empty Dashboard/Desk.)*

**"Let me show you. Here is 'The Digital Desk'. When a meeting ends, you simply drag and drop the `.txt` or `.vtt` file right in."**

*(Drag and drop `01_Bug_Triage`, `04_Q1_Strategy`, and `05_Board_Update` into the file zone. Wait 1 second for them to pop up as Post-It notes.)*

**"Notice how fast that was. Under the hood, we are using the Groq Llama 3.3 model to perform blazing-fast NLP extraction. It reads the transcript, finds the sentiment, logs decisions, and extracts tasks with specific owners and due dates."**

---

### Phase 2: Actionable Task Board (0:30 - 1:00)
*(Click on the 'Tasks' link in the top navigation bar.)*

**"But extracting tasks is only half the battle; tracking them is what matters across an organization."**

**"Our Master Action Board aggregates every single task assigned across every meeting into one centralized Kanban checklist. As you can see, Sarah and David's assignments from different meetings are completely organized. As a manager, I can track execution effortlessly."**

*(Click a few checkboxes so they move to the 'Completed' section!)*

---

### Phase 3: Workspace Analytics (1:00 - 1:30)
*(Click on the 'Health' link in the top navigation bar.)*

**"And because our AI quantifies everything it reads, we can use tools like Recharts to visualize the un-visualizable."**

**"This is the Workspace Health Dashboard. On the left, we map Team Morale. Notice the severe dip here? If you hover over it, you'll see this was our 'Bug Triage' emergency meeting. But shortly after, we had our 'Board Update' meeting, and morale skyrocketed to 90%."**

*(Hover over the Bar Chart on the right)*

**"On the right, we have Action Item Distribution. I can instantly see who is carrying the heaviest workload across the entire company."**

---

### Phase 4: The 'Wow' Factor (RAG Chatbot) (1:30 - 2:00)
*(Click the 'Desk' link to return to the main hub. Click the floating Message icon in the bottom right to open "Workspace QUAN".)*

**"Finally, we wanted to ensure organizations never lose their historical context. So, we built a True Vector RAG architecture directly into the browser."**

**"When a transcript is uploaded, we use Google Gemini Text Embeddings to mathematically chunk the file. That allows our AI Assistant, QUAN, to answer complex, cross-referenced questions."**

*(Type this into the chat and hit enter)*:
`"I know we canceled our paid ads. Why did we do that, and what are David and Sarah building as a replacement?"` 

*(Wait for QUAN to answer.)*

**"QUAN just used Cosine Similarity math to retrieve the precise transcript chunks from our Q1 Strategy meeting, and effortlessly synthesized the answer: Michael cut our ads due to budget constraints, and David and Sarah are executing a new Referral Program."**

**"QUANTIFY doesn't just record your meetings. It finally puts them to work."**
